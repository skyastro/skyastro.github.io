Star data: GAIA DR3 Nearby Star Catalog supplemented by SIMBAD query (January 2022) for stars within 100 pc to ensure inclusion of bright stars. When galaxy=small Extended Hipparcos Catalog queried at Vizier.

Abell Clusters: Abell ZCAT catalog (queried at HEASARC/Xamin)
